Pune Startup Fest

The Mega Second Edition

BHAU’s Entrepreneurship Cell of College of Engineering, Pune (CoEP) and Polycab India Limited presented the second edition of Pune Startup Fest- ‘Pune Startup Fest’20’, in association with Bank of Maharashtra, powered by FinIQ and under the patronage of Startup India, Skill India, MHRD’s Innovation Cell and in collaboration with Pune Municipal Corporation on 15th February, 2020(Saturday) and 16th February, 2020(Sunday) at College of Engineering, Pune. Pune Startup Fest’20, with the theme – ‘Discovering the known unknowns’, aimed to bring together 130+ innovative startups through a startup expo, along with investors, mentors, C-Level executives from top corporations, industry and general public across the country and successfully epitomized this idea over the two grand days of the fest. Pune Startup Fest’20 was a conclave of 132 participant startups, the zone-wise breakup of which was 23 Social startups, 19 Health & Lifestyle startups, 12 Agriculture startups, 63 Technical startups, 10 Student startups, 5 Innovation-zone startups; 90 investors and mentors; 5 entrepreneurship-related events and other activities.

Pune Startup Fest’20 provided

  • A platform for entrepreneurs to connect and compete with each other.
  • Investor zone of more than 90 investors and mentors.
  • Student startups a platform to display their innovative ideas.
  • Innovation zone for forthcoming startups.
  • Students’ Internship zone - internship opportunities for students in startups.
  • Success stories and keynote of well-known entrepreneurs and personalities to guide students and startups.
  • Panel Discussions among eminent panelists for startups, students and general public.
  • Entrepreneurship-related events: Bidmaster, Bullrun, Brandscan, Baggage Battles and BudgeIt for college students.

Case Study

Case Study' was organised on 24th September 2019. In Case Study, the members were divided in different groups each, assigned one company from the industry. All these companies were on the top of their game but failed to maintain their position. Each group researched the company, the reasons for their failure and came up with a feasible solution on how they could rebuild their company. Students did great research and came up with excellent solutions and presented it in front of our judges. The finale was judged by Mr. Vijay Talele, Mr Girish and Dr. Madhuri Karnik.

B-Plan

B-Plan: Business Plan competition was organised by Bhau’s E-Cell for interested COEP Students in December 2019. The teams from COEP including E-Cell members enthusiastically participated in B-plan competition. Investors and judges were happy with the participation and enthralled with the quality of B-Plans presented in front of them. The winners for B-plan will be declared in Pune Startup Fest’20 and will be awarded.

Field Visit

Field visit: Bhau’s E-Cell organised a field visit of the Bhau building. Students from our college were taken on a field trip of the Bhau Building in College of Engineering Pune. They were given an opportunity to explore an Incubation centre. They learned about the startup culture and gained knowledge of working of the startups and Bhau as an Incubation centre.
