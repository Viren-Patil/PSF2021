About Startup Expo

The Start-up Expo is one of the key highlights of Pune Startup Fest. The Expo is aimed at providing entrepreneurs a high visibility transformative platform where they can showcase their ventures, meet investors, engage with government organizations, attend knowledge sessions, receive one-on-one mentoring, network with business experts, explore partnership opportunities, find potential customers and interact with students and stakeholders that can help them scale new heights. It serves as a great medium of interaction between the founders and students, thus promoting a thriving entrepreneurial culture. The start-ups also recruit these young innovators who look out for new opportunities.

Key Highlights



Start-up Expo

  • --> 133 start-ups that provided over 500+ internship opportunities.
  • --> 8 separate zones to explore: Technical zone, Student startup zone, Agriculture zone, Social zone, Health & Lifestyle zone, Innovation zone, Breaking the Pandemic (startups helping COVID 19 situation) and Incubators, accelerators, financial institutes and Co-Working spaces.

Startup Zones


Social Startups

Features startups emphasizing on solution to a social problem to create change.

Agricultural Startups

Features startups that help enhance traditional farming ways or develop new ones.

Technical Startups

Features products and services of startups which leverages technology.

Health & Lifestyle Startups

Features startups that aim to rejuvenate human life.

Student Startups

Features Young entrepeneurs striving to create a difference

Other Startups

Incubators, accelerators, financial institutions and coworking spaces.

Innovation Zone

Featuring creations of the innovators with IPR and/or patents with the sole aim of solving widespread problems.

Breaking the Pandemic

Featuring startups that helped overcome COVID-19 restrictions.

Investor Zone

The busiest room at Pune Startup Fest’20 by BHAU’s Entrepreneurship Cell of the College of Engineering Pune (COEP) was the ‘Investor Zone’.

Pune Startup Fest’20 was cherished by a very warm response. About 85+ investors and mentors visited the event in 2 days. They interacted with the startups at their stalls as per their domain of interest. Further, they were invited at BHAU building wherein arrangements were made for one on one interaction with the startups. Our moto of providing investments was acheived when startups recieved investments of over 3.5 crore alongwith mentoring. With all of this taking place the investors zone became as definite centre of attraction for the fest as mentioned by the Hindustan Times.
