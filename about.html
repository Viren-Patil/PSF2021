About COEP

Pune is called as the ‘Oxford of the East’, as it is a hub to many educational institutes. College of Engineering, Pune (COEP) is a prominent educational institute in Pune as it is the 3rd oldest engineering college in Asia. Established in 1854, this institute is a 165-year-old legacy. COEP has many notable alumni from different walks of life like Bharat Ratna Mr. M Visvesvaraya, Prof. Thomas Kailath, Dr. C. K. N. Patel and many more to name. COEP strives for the overall development of its students. With outstanding curriculum, COEP provides various co-curricular and extracurricular activities. With its strong history and foundation, COEP continues to shape exceptional engineers, innovators, entrepreneurs and personalities from the students.

About BHAU's Entrepreneurship Cell

The Bhau’s E-Cell of CoEP was established in 2006. The cell has stimulated, supported and sustained Entrepreneurial ventures within the campus. Various activities are held throughout the year to inculcate entrepreneurship among the students. It aims to undertake various initiatives through which students can access relevant entrepreneurial resources, network with prominent entrepreneurs and build an incredible relationship with the investors with dedicated and full-fledged support from Bhau’s Institute Of Entrepreneurship. Every year E-cell organises keynote lectures, competitions, workshops and various other events which introduces young technocrats to various management practices in the corporate world.

About Pune Startup Fest'20

The second edition of Pune StartUp Fest was organised on 15th and 16th of February 2020. PSF'20 with a befitting theme "Discovering the known Unkowns", gave all the students a great platform to explore their entrepreneurial sides. PSF'20 was under the patronage of start-up India and in association with JioGenNext. PSF’20 was a huge success with a footfall of about 15000+ throughout the two days.
